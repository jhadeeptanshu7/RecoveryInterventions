Welcome to SMARTS

SMARTS (Social Media based drug Addiction Recovery intervention Targetting web Server) is a web server that utilizes supervised machine learning, natural language processing, and causal analysis techniques to automatically determine high yield responders to drug addiction recovery interventions. SMARTS can further assist the epidemiologists and public health officials in analyzing the sentiment of the posts, identifying the drug, and recovery terms, different topics, and the location information present in the posts of the users. SMARTS is part of our effort to provide publicly available technologies that can be used by epidemiologists to identify individuals open to drug addiction recovery interventions and help curb the opioid epidemic.
